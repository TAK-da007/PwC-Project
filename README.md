## 📞 Call Centre Performance Analysis — PWC Project  

### 🎯 **Project Objective:**  
The goal of this project was to analyze call center performance data to uncover key insights that improve operational efficiency, agent productivity, and customer satisfaction.  

---

### 🔍 **Key Performance Metrics:**  
- **Average Handle Time (AHT):** Tracked how efficiently agents resolved customer issues.  
- **First Call Resolution (FCR):** Measured the percentage of issues resolved on the first call, ensuring service quality.  
- **Call Abandonment Rate:** Identified how many customers abandoned calls due to long wait times.  
- **Service Level (SL):** Assessed how quickly calls were answered, ensuring responsiveness.  
- **Agent Utilization Rate:** Monitored how effectively agents used their time between calls and post-call tasks.  
- **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):** Evaluated overall customer happiness based on feedback.  

---

### 📊 **Tools & Techniques Used:**  
- **Power BI:** Built interactive dashboards and visualizations to present data insights.  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Created dynamic measures and calculated metrics for deeper analysis.  
- **Data Cleaning & Transformation:** Ensured accurate reporting through data preparation in Power BI.  

---

### 🚀 **Project Outcome:**  
- **Identified peak call times** and optimized staffing to reduce call wait times.  
- **Highlighted underperforming agents** and recommended tailored coaching plans.  
- **Improved first-call resolution rates**, driving better customer experiences.  
- **Delivered a comprehensive dashboard** for continuous performance monitoring.

## 🔍 Churn Dashboard & Customer Risk Analysis  

### 🎯 **Project Objective:**  
The goal of this project was to analyze customer behavior, identify churn patterns, and assess customer risk to help improve retention strategies and business performance.  

---

### 📊 **Key Insights Delivered:**  
- **Churn Rate Analysis:** Measured the percentage of customers leaving over a given period.  
- **Customer Segmentation:** Categorized customers by demographics, tenure, and service usage to spot high-risk groups.  
- **Behavioral Patterns:** Analyzed usage patterns (e.g., "Multiple Lines," "Internet Service") to detect early churn signals.  
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):** Predicted potential revenue loss from churning customers.  

---

### 🔥 **Key Metrics Tracked:**  
- **Overall Churn Rate:** Percentage of customers lost.  
- **Retention Rate:** Measures how many customers stayed.  
- **High-Risk Customer Score:** Weighted analysis of customer features indicating a likelihood to churn (e.g., contract type, monthly charges, support calls).  
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):** Impact analysis on revenue loss due to churn.  

---

### 🛠️ **Tools & Techniques Used:**  
- **Power BI:** Created dynamic visualizations and interactive dashboards.  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Built custom measures like `%Yes-Multiple_Lines` to track churn-specific behaviors.  
- **SQL:** Cleaned, transformed, and merged datasets for effective analysis.  
- **Python (Pandas/Matplotlib):** Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) and statistical insights.  

---

### 🚀 **Project Outcome:**  
- **Identified high-risk customer segments** prone to churn, enabling targeted retention efforts.  
- **Predicted potential revenue loss**, helping prioritize customers for proactive engagement.  
- **Created an actionable dashboard** for ongoing churn monitoring and performance tracking.  
- **Recommended strategies** (e.g., personalized offers, enhanced support) to improve retention.

## 🌍 **Diversity & Inclusion Project Overview**  

### 🎯 **Project Objective:**  
The goal of this project was to analyze workforce diversity, identify representation gaps, and measure the effectiveness of inclusion initiatives — fostering a more balanced, equitable, and inclusive workplace.  

---

### 🔍 **Key Focus Areas:**  
- **Workforce Demographics:** Analyzed gender, ethnicity, age, and other diversity dimensions across departments and roles.  
- **Representation Gaps:** Identified underrepresented groups and areas needing improved diversity efforts.  
- **Inclusion Sentiment Analysis:** Measured employee sentiment and engagement to assess inclusion effectiveness.  
- **Hiring & Promotion Patterns:** Examined recruitment, promotions, and retention data to ensure fair opportunities.  

---

### 📊 **Key Metrics Tracked:**  
- **Diversity Ratio:** Percentage of employees across various demographic groups.  
- **Leadership Diversity:** Representation of diverse groups in leadership and senior roles.  
- **Hiring Equity:** Ratio of diverse candidates hired and promoted vs. total hires.  
- **Inclusion Score:** Based on employee surveys, tracking feelings of belonging and fair treatment.  
- **Retention Rate by Group:** Monitored retention and turnover rates across different demographics.  

---

### 🛠️ **Tools & Techniques Used:**  
- **Power BI / Tableau:** Built interactive dashboards for easy representation of diversity metrics.  
- **SQL:** Extracted and joined HR datasets to ensure complete workforce data analysis.  
- **Python (Pandas):** Cleaned, processed, and analyzed large datasets for in-depth insights.  
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Used text analysis to extract insights from employee feedback surveys.  

---

### 🚀 **Project Outcome:**  
- **Identified representation gaps** across departments and leadership roles.  
- **Provided actionable insights** to improve hiring, retention, and promotion practices.  
- **Built a real-time diversity dashboard** to track and monitor inclusion progress.  
- **Recommended strategies** — like bias training, mentorship programs, and equitable hiring practices — to improve workplace diversity and foster an inclusive culture.
